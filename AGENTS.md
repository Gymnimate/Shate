# Shate Applet Instructions

Core design decisions and constraints defined by the user that must be strictly respected in all future updates:

## Panel Navigation Rule
- **No clickable buttons or tabs for panels**: There must be NO panel indicator dots, navigation tabs, or button-based panel switchers (e.g. "Ranní Routine", "Denní Úkoly", "Večerní Routine") at the top or bottom of the cards.
- **Swipe-Only or Voice controls**: Switching between the three panels (Morning routine, Daily plan, Evening routine) must be achieved exclusively through touch swipe gestures (left/right) or voice commands with Shate.
- **Clean Display Card layout**: Keep the checklist card container visually plain, centered, and clean, with its focus entirely on the rendered active checklist without added tab bar clutter.
